Heath and Deborah Campbell, parents of ‘Adolf Hitler’, lost custody of their fourth child within eighteen hours of its birth.

The New Jersey Division of Youth and Family Services took into custody seventeen hour old Hons Campbell following a call from the doctor who helped deliver him late Thursday.

Their three children, all named after Nazi inspirations, were taken into custody by the state suthorities. The children were named Adolf Hitler Campbell, JoyceLynn Aryan Nation Campbell and Honszlynn Himler Jeannie Campbell. Since that time in January of 2009, the three kids have remained in foster care.
