"V is for Vengeance" illustrates Grafton's skill as a solid plotter. The breathless story takes several logical twists that lead to a believable finale. With just a few letters left, "V is for Vengeance" proves that Grafton is making the most of them.

"V is for Vengeance" finds Kinsey a few weeks shy of her 38th birthday, which affords the detective a chance to look at her life. The pending birthday also propels the decidedly unfashionable Kinsey to shop in Nordstrom's lingerie department. The idea that Kinsey, whose wardrobe consists of jeans, turtleneck sweaters and one black dress, would venture to Nordstrom's is mind-boggling, and Kinsey is seduced by the store and one terrific sale.
